You enter the unknown room and find piles of junk all around. Is it possible you have entered a new dimension? 
One where this junk is riches to a monster of unbelievable size. Or maybe you have stumbled upon a nest 
belonging to a subterrainian race of rat people that use McMillan as a feeding ground. 
Or, more likely, people just leave their junk out and it is thrown in here and forgotton. 
You prefer the first and second options.
Press 1 to search for the rat people.
Press 2 to return to entrance hall.
Press 0 to end the game.
